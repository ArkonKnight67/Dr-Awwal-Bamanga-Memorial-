A Life of Love and Inspiration
My father was a man of great strength, wisdom, and kindness. He touched the lives of everyone around him with his generosity and unwavering support. His laughter filled our home, and his guidance shaped who I am today.
Who He Was & Academic Background
Dr. Awwal Bamanga was a marine environmental scientist, specializing in environmental sciences and sediment geochemistry.

Education:
  • B.Sc in Biomedical Sciences, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria (2000)
  • Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) in Health, Safety and Environmental Management, University of Lagos
  • M.Sc in Environmental Management / Environmental Sciences, University of Lagos
  • PhD in Marine Environmental Sciences (specializing in marine sediments and sediment geochemistry), University of Portsmouth, UK (2015)
His Work & Impact
At NIMASA (Nigerian Maritime Administration & Safety Agency), he served as Assistant Director in the Marine Environment Management Department for over 21 years. His work focused on regulating shipping activities to ensure safer, cleaner oceans under international conventions and protocols. He monitored marine pollution, protected the environment, and helped Nigeria meet its obligations in marine transport.

He was also a Visiting Lecturer at the International Maritime Law Institute (IMLI), sharing his knowledge and passion for marine science with students.
Key Research & Projects
  • Heavy Metal Sediment Geochemistry in Lagos Harbour: Investigated how heavy metals accumulate in harbor sediments and their impact on water quality, human health, and marine life.
  • Ballast Water Sediment Studies: Researched the chemical composition of ballast water sediments in ships docked in Lagos Harbour, addressing pollution and ecosystem health.
  • Blue Economy Initiatives: Delivered papers and led discussions on balancing economic growth with ocean health, including a notable presentation on World Ocean Day.
